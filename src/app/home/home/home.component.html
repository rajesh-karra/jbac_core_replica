Life Enlightenment Foundation (LEF)


LEF Vision :

Serving Christian Community and Unite Christian Community to Serve the AP State.


LEF Mission :

1. Bringing all the believers into single platform including labour to landlord and attender to IAS, Advocates, Doctors, Engineers, Officers, Politicians, Pastors, Business Men, Students & all professionals, so that helping and supporting each other.

2. Unite the whole Chritian Community irrespective of Denomination and Caste.

3. The Jesus Believers Association Council (JBAC) shall be constituted to oversee the mission of LEF and activities of the association, with its members renewed every two years. The council shall be responsible for executing and advancing the mission of the Foundation.
